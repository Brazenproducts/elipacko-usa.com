FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from US buyers evaluating PP corrugated packaging.

What is PP corrugated plastic and how is it different from cardboard?
PP corrugated (twin-wall polypropylene) is made from two parallel PP skins connected by internal fluted channels. Unlike cardboard corrugated, PP is 100% waterproof — it never absorbs moisture, sags, or loses structural integrity when wet. It is reusable 200–500 trips vs single-use cardboard, and FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 food-safe for direct food contact applications.
Does Elipacko have anti-dumping duties on US imports?
No. Elipacko products are manufactured outside China and enter the US at 0% anti-dumping duty. Chinese corrugated plastic manufacturers are subject to 25%+ Section 301 tariffs. That means a direct 25% landed cost advantage vs Chinese suppliers — before freight savings from door-to-door delivery.
Are Elipacko products FDA food-safe?
Yes. All Elipacko PP packaging complies with FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 for direct food contact. Products are also USDA accepted for food facility use. No additional certification is required — buyers can reference FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 in their facility documentation.
What is the minimum order quantity?
500 pieces for most standard products. One full 40HQ container can be produced in 3 days. Custom sizes and tooling may require 14–21 days for the first run. Contact Elipacko for specific MOQ on specialty products like meat lugs and poultry crates.
Can I get custom sizes, colors, and printing?
Yes — all of the above. Elipacko's engineering team provides free CAD design, custom printing (UV and screen print, 4-color), and prototype samples before production. Custom colors are available at standard MOQ with lead time 14–21 days for first run.
How do PP pallets compare to wood pallets?
PP pallets are ISPM 15 exempt (no fumigation required for export), lighter (18–28 lb vs 40–70 lb for wood), pest-proof, and USDA-accepted for food facilities. They are rackable to 2,900 lb and have a 500+ trip cycle life. For food facility audits, PP pallets eliminate the contamination risks flagged by USDA inspectors for wood pallets.
What ports does Elipacko deliver to?
Direct door-to-door shipments to Los Angeles (LA/Long Beach), New Jersey (Port Newark/Elizabeth), and Savannah (Port of Savannah). Full logistics coordination is included in the quote — no need to arrange separate freight forwarding.
