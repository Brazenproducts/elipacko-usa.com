PP vs Wood Poultry Crates

USDA food facility approved

Wood poultry crates harbor Salmonella, Campylobacter, and other pathogens in grain structure and splinter points. USDA-inspected facilities increasingly mandate non-wood contact surfaces. PP crates are USDA-accepted and cleanable to food facility standards.

Pressure washable

  • Hot water washdown rated to 140°F
  • Compatible with quaternary ammonium, chlorine, and acid-based sanitizers
  • No grain absorption — complete surface sanitization

NCC compliance

National Chicken Council transport guidelines specify minimum ventilation, stocking density, and handling requirements. Elipacko PP poultry boxes are designed to NCC standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these NCC compliant?

Yes. Elipacko PP poultry boxes meet National Chicken Council transport guidelines for vent area, stocking density, and handling requirements.

Do they work for live bird transport?

Yes — live bird crates have full ventilation on all four sides plus top. Smooth interior reduces wing and leg injuries during transport.

What sanitizers are compatible?

Compatible with quaternary ammonium compounds, sodium hypochlorite (chlorine), peracetic acid, and citric acid sanitizers standard in poultry processing.

Can I stack these 8 high in a refrigerated truck?

Yes — rated for 8-high loaded stack. Verify with Elipacko that your specific load weight is within the stack rating for your box size.
