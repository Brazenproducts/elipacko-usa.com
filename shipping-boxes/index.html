Key Features

What Makes PP Corrugated Shipping Boxes Different

💧

100% Waterproof

PP corrugated is non-absorbent. Rain, condensation, or spills do not compromise box integrity. No wet-stack failures, no delamination, no collapsed corners.

🔄

Reusable 10–50 Cycles

A PP corrugated shipping box typically survives 10–50 round trips in a closed-loop operation. Single-use cardboard cannot be reused at all. The per-trip cost advantage compounds quickly at volume.

⚖️

Same Weight, Higher Strength

PP corrugated is lighter than equivalent-wall cardboard corrugated, but maintains rated compression strength in wet, humid, and cold conditions where cardboard fails.

🔒

Tamper-Evident Options

Clip-lock and adhesive-seal closure options provide tamper evidence for pharmaceutical, electronics, and high-value goods shipping applications.

🎨

Custom Print & Color

Full-color print on PP corrugated for branded unboxing, retail-ready mailers, or product identification at the distribution center. No label required.

♻️

Fully Recyclable

PP corrugated is 100% recyclable through closed-loop PP recovery programs. At end-of-life it goes back into new PP pellets. No contaminated wax or mixed-material waste stream.

Use Cases

Who Uses Reusable PP Shipping Boxes

Grocery & Food Distribution

Hub-to-store distribution networks with return-loop logistics benefit most from PP corrugated. Boxes ship loaded to the store and return empty — the same unit circulating indefinitely rather than being crushed and baled at each destination.

E-Commerce Fulfillment (Closed Loop)

Subscription box operations, rental model e-commerce, and B2B fulfillment operations with predictable return flows. The customer returns the box, it's washed and re-packed. The branded PP mailer becomes a physical part of the product experience rather than disposable waste.

Automotive & Industrial Parts

Parts packaging for just-in-time automotive supply chains. PP corrugated boxes are chemical-resistant, stackable to specification, and maintain compression strength under rack storage. The same box ships with parts, returns empty, and ships again.

Pharmaceutical & Medical

Temperature-controlled shipping with insulated liner inserts. PP corrugated is compatible with dry-ice and gel-pack cold chain. Non-absorbent surface meets clean-room handling requirements and supports tamper-evident closure options.

FAQs

Shipping Box Questions

Can PP corrugated boxes be used with standard carrier labels?

Yes. Standard thermal-transfer and laser shipping labels adhere to PP corrugated surfaces. For high-volume applications, labels can be pre-applied at the factory, or a label area can be printed directly on the box surface. Label adhesion is better than on wax-coated cardboard surfaces.

What closure types are available?

Standard RSC (regular slotted container) fold-and-close, snap-lock/clip-lock, adhesive-strip tear-open, and zipper-tear reseal. Tamper-evident closure with security seal is available for pharmaceutical and high-value goods applications.

How are the boxes washed and sanitised between cycles?

High-pressure wash with standard food-facility or industrial sanitisers. PP corrugated withstands steam cleaning up to 212°F. The non-absorbent surface means no residue or bacterial harbour sites remain after a standard wash. Wash equipment for returned boxes can be as simple as a pressure washer and drying rack.

Are custom dimensions available or only standard sizes?

Custom dimensions are Elipacko's default — most customers specify their exact interior dimension requirement and Elipacko designs the box blank to match. Standard sizes are available for faster delivery when an existing spec matches your requirement.
