Applications

What PP Corrugated Sheets Are Used For

📦

Packaging Inserts & Dividers

Layer pads, cell dividers, corner protectors, and slip sheets. Cut to exact box interior dimensions. Lightweight, reusable, no moisture absorption.

🪧

Signage & Real Estate Signs

4mm corrugated flute is the standard substrate for yard signs, political signs, and retail POP displays. Full-color print direct or print-and-apply.

🌱

Agriculture & Greenhouse

Fluted sheets for seedling trays, greenhouse cladding, and produce box blanks. UV-stabilized grades rated for 3–5 years outdoor exposure.

🏗️

Construction Protection

Floor protection panels, surface guards, and temporary walkways during construction. 6–10mm sheets resist foot traffic, carts, and light equipment.

🧊

Cold Chain & Refrigeration

Insulated panel liners, cold room dividers, and refrigerated display inserts. PP retains structural integrity from −40°F to 212°F.

🚛

Automotive & Industrial

Parts tray liners, pallet separators, and component packaging. Chemical-resistant surface handles oils, solvents, and cleaning agents without degradation.

Material Properties

Why Polypropylene Corrugated

vs. Cardboard Corrugated

  • 100% waterproof — no moisture absorption
  • Reusable 10–50× vs single-use cardboard
  • Chemical resistant — unaffected by most solvents
  • Consistent compression strength when wet
  • 100% recyclable, closed-loop PP recovery

vs. Solid Plastic Sheet

PP corrugated sheet weighs 70–80% less than solid PP sheet of equivalent thickness. The fluted structure provides disproportionate rigidity relative to weight — ideal for packaging and signage where every gram of freight cost matters.

FAQs

PP Corrugated Sheet Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for custom-cut sheets?

Minimum order is typically one 40HQ container. For standard sizes and thicknesses, Elipacko may be able to fill from stock or combine with other orders. Contact us with your spec and volume — we'll confirm MOQ and lead time at the quoting stage.

Can sheets be supplied pre-scored or pre-cut for box blanks?

Yes. Elipacko can supply sheets pre-scored to a fold line pattern so they assemble into boxes at your facility without additional cutting equipment. Provide your box blank die-line and we'll match it. This is common for produce box blanks and shipping box operations.

Are anti-static or ESD grades available?

Yes. Anti-static (surface resistivity 10⁶–10⁹ Ω) and conductive (10³–10⁵ Ω) grades are available in black corrugated sheet. These are used in electronics assembly packaging, component tray liners, and PCB shipping containers where electrostatic discharge is a concern.

What is the lead time from order to US port delivery?

Production of one 40HQ container is typically 3 days. Sea freight transit to US West Coast ports (LA/Long Beach) runs 14–21 days. Total order-to-dock time for a standard spec is approximately 3–4 weeks. Elipacko will confirm exact lead time at the quoting stage based on your spec and destination port.

Is there anti-dumping duty on PP corrugated sheets from China?

No. PP corrugated sheets are not subject to anti-dumping duty entering the United States. The 0% ADD rate applies to polypropylene corrugated sheets — confirm the HTS code for your specific spec with your customs broker. Elipacko provides the country of origin documentation required for entry.
