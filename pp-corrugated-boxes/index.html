Why PP Over Cardboard

No more box collapse

Cardboard absorbs moisture and loses structural integrity in humid warehouses and refrigerated environments. PP corrugated boxes are 100% waterproof — they never sag, delaminate, or fail due to liquid exposure.

Real reuse economics

At $3–6 per cardboard box vs $0.10–0.30 per PP trip amortized over 300 cycles, the math is clear. A fleet of 500 PP boxes saves $13,500–$28,500 over 300 trips.

Frequently Asked Questions

What wall thickness should I order?

4 mm for light produce under 30 lb. 6 mm for standard 44–66 lb loads. 8 mm for heavy industrial and bulk applications over 66 lb.

Are these FDA food-safe?

Yes. All Elipacko PP corrugated boxes comply with FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 for direct food contact.

Can I get custom printing?

Yes — UV print and screen print available. Custom colors, logos, and product information on all panels.

What is the minimum order quantity?

500 pieces for standard sizes. Custom sizes may have higher MOQ depending on tooling requirements.
