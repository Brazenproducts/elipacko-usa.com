Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a gaylord box and a bulk container?

Gaylord is an industry term for a pallet-scale bulk box — typically 48×40 in footprint, used for bulk industrial and agricultural product. The terms are interchangeable.

Do PP gaylord boxes need a liner?

For fine powders or liquids, a PE bag liner is recommended. For solid produce and industrial parts, no liner is needed.

Can they be washed between cycles?

Yes — pressure washable. Compatible with standard SDS cleaning protocols used in food facilities.

What is the minimum order?

250 pieces for standard sizes. Custom dimensions available at higher MOQ.
