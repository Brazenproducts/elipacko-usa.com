Why Switch

PP vs Wax Cartons for Seafood

Moisture & Contamination

Wax cartons absorb seafood liquids through the wax layer within a single use. The absorbed fluid is a bacterial growth medium. PP corrugated is 100% non-absorbent — all surface moisture is external and removable with a rinse.

Cold Chain Integrity

Wax cartons lose 30–40% of their compression strength when wet. A stack of wax cartons with ice and seafood moisture will deform under load. PP boxes maintain rated compression strength at −40°F, wet or dry, through the full cold chain cycle.

Export Compliance

Wax cartons classified as wood-composite packaging may require ISPM 15 documentation in certain export markets. PP corrugated contains no wood fiber — it is ISPM 15 exempt, requiring no fumigation or heat-treatment certificates for international shipment.

Total Cost Per Trip

A PP corrugated seafood box reused 10 times across a closed-loop operation costs approximately one-third the per-trip cost of single-use wax cartons at scale. Elipacko can model the breakeven point for your volume — contact us for a cost comparison.

FAQs

Seafood Packaging Questions

Can PP corrugated boxes be used with ice directly?

Yes. PP is non-absorbent and unaffected by prolonged ice contact. Ice melt drains through drainage channels in the base design, or can be retained with a sealed-base option depending on your application. The box surface does not degrade from ice contact over multiple cycles.

Are the boxes food-grade and FDA compliant?

Yes. Elipacko seafood boxes use food-grade polypropylene with FDA-compliant surface formulation. No plasticisers or additives that would require additional food-contact clearance are used in the standard seafood box range.

How are the boxes cleaned between uses?

High-pressure wash with standard food-facility sanitisers. PP corrugated withstands temperatures up to 212°F (100°C) steam cleaning. The non-absorbent surface means no residual odor or bacterial harbour sites remain after a standard CIP wash cycle.

What formats are available for live seafood transport?

Ventilated crate formats with drainage base channels are available for live crab, lobster, and shellfish. Ventilation opening percentage is specified to maintain oxygen flow rates for the specific species and transport duration. Contact Elipacko with species and transit time to get the right spec.
