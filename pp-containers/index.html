Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a tote and a container?

In most industrial contexts they're interchangeable. Tote typically implies a smaller, hand-carry or pallet-jack size. Container refers to larger, forklift-moved units. Elipacko makes both.

Can I get custom colors for visual sorting?

Yes — custom colors are available at standard MOQ. Color coding helps visual sort at distribution centers.

Are these compatible with automated conveyor systems?

Custom base profiles can be designed for conveyor compatibility. Contact Elipacko with your conveyor specifications.

What cleaning chemicals can I use?

PP has excellent chemical resistance. Compatible with most food-safe sanitizers, degreasers, and alkaline cleaners. Avoid concentrated oxidizing acids.
